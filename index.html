Trellis

Inspired by the tile-laying game: grow a shared garden of hex tiles, bloom vines where colors meet, then place a flower on your new tile on any bloomed edge. First to place all flowers wins. Hotseat 2-player — select a hand tile, press R to rotate it, click a highlighted empty hex to plant, then click a glowing edge on that tile for your flower.
