# Design System

Welcome to our Design System, where consistency meets creativity. This document outlines the core principles and specifications that guide the visual and interactive elements of our products.

## Theme Overview

Our design language is built on a `light` color mode, ensuring clarity and readability across all interfaces.

### Color Palette

The chosen color palette is designed to be `vibrant`, creating an energetic and engaging user experience.

-   **Primary Color**: `#0082fd` - This bright blue serves as our brand's most distinctive color, ideal for prominent calls to action and interactive elements.
-   **Secondary Color**: `#5071d5` - A supportive, slightly muted blue-violet, perfect for less prominent UI elements and secondary actions.
-   **Tertiary Color**: `#a459b5` - An accent purple-pink for highlights, badges, or decorative elements, adding a touch of distinctiveness.
-   **Neutral Color**: `#6a769e` - A balanced neutral tone used for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, providing a stable foundation.

### Typography

Our typographic system is designed for modern readability and clear hierarchy.

-   **Headline Font**: `Inter` - Used for all headlines, ensuring strong visual impact and readability at larger sizes.
-   **Body Font**: `Inter` - Applied to all body text, chosen for its excellent legibility across various screen sizes and contexts.
-   **Label Font**: `Inter` - Utilized for labels and smaller text elements, maintaining consistency with our primary fonts.

### Shape and Form

The geometry of our UI elements contributes significantly to the overall feel of the product.

-   **Roundedness**: `2` - We opt for a moderate level of roundedness on UI shapes, striking a balance between modern softness and clear definition.

### Spacing

Consistent spacing is crucial for a clean and organized layout.

-   **Spacing**: `2` - A normal level of whitespace is maintained within and between UI elements, ensuring a balanced and comfortable visual experience.
